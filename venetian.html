Venetian Plaster

Venetian plaster is a decorative finish applied to walls and ceilings that can be manipulated to achieve the most eye catching, stunning innovative designs and colour combinations to suit any atmosphere be it your home, commercial or workplace.

Using the finest materials imported all the way from Italy each design is bespoke and tailored to your taste and requirements making it the perfect option to add a high quality flare to any interior space as no two designs can ever be produced the same.

Venetian plaster is always a major talking point and can be applied by our team of expert artisans to suit any environment whether its at home to impress your guests or blow your clients away at your place of business, Venetian plaster is the perfect solution.
