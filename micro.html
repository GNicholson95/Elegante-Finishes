Micro cement

Micro cement is an extremely versatile cement based coating that can be applied smooth or textured in a vast range of colours and finished with a matte, satin or glossy sealant making for timeless, luxurious and classy designs.

With a thickness of approximately 2mm microcement is extremely hard wearing and fully waterproof making it an excellent choice for high traffic areas such as walls, floors, stairs, bathrooms, kitchens, swimming pools and exteriors.

Using the most advanced micro cement on the market imported from Spain Topcret is 3X stronger the conventional microcememts and has achieved the highest ranking on Leed, Bream and Well certifications.

Its high resistance (120N/MM2*) allows for prolonged use and lifespan not only in dwellings but for commercial units and stores, public spaces and any other high foot traffic areas providing the ultimate longevity.
